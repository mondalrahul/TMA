Dr. Khaimov completed medical school at the prestigious Yale University in New Haven, Connecticut. He completed his training in Anesthesiology at Columbia University New York Presbyterian Hospital. Fellowship in Pain Management followed at Beth Israel Medical Center under the guidance of world renowned experts in pain medicine including Dr. Ressell Portenoy and Dr. Suelane Do Ouro.  He specializes in minimally invasive treatments for many painful conditions. A prompt diagnosis and a targeted treatment allow the patient to return to normal activity in a short period of time. In most cases his treatments avoid invasive surgery for the patient.
