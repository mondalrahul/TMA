Dr. Vanessa McDade Drake D.D.S. (Moyes) earned a Bachelor of Science degree in Biology at the University of Tennessee, Chattanooga. She then went on to receive her Doctorate of Dental Science at the University of Tennessee, College of Dentistry at Memphis in 2006. Dr. Moyes is a member of the American Dental Association, Tennessee Dental Association, and the Chattanooga Area Dental Society. She is also a member of the Dental Organization for Conscious Sedation, The Chattanooga Dental Implant Society and is trained in other advanced procedures such as Invisalign. Dr. Drake has two children and enjoys being involved in her church. She is a native of Harrison, TN and has had the privilege of practicing dentistry in Chattanooga for several years.
