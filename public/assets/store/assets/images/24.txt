Grace Darnell is a board-certified nurse practitioner at Goshen Center for Cancer Care. She provides care for women with complex issues related to cancer and other conditions of the reproductive system.{line break}In addition to gynecological exams and screenings, Grace assists with minimally invasive procedures, including laparoscopic surgeries and treatment for secondary malignancies. She has more than a decade of experience in oncology units and infusion centers and holds advanced certification in treating cancer through chemotherapy. Grace also provides acute care for oncology patients admitted to the hospital.
