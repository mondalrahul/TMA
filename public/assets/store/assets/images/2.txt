A 1984 graduate of the California College of Podiatric Medicine, in San Francisco, California, he has practiced the art and science of medical and surgical foot and ankle care for over thirty years. After completing his residency in podiatry and foot surgery at the Veteran's Administration Medical Center in Seattle, Washington, he moved back to his hometown of San Mateo, California and started practice. Dr. Passeri combines the latest medical and surgical advances in foot care and foot surgery with a time honored approach to good patient care; communication and full time and attention to the patient's needs and concerns. Dr. Kenneth Passeri is board certified by the American Board of Foot and Ankle Surgery and the American Board of Podiatric Orthopedics and Primary Podiatric Medicine. He is a Fellow of the American College of Foot and Ankle Surgeons. Dr. Passeri is a member of the American Podiatric Medical Association and the California Podiatric Medical Association. He is on the Medical Staff of Mills-Peninsula Hospital, and San Ramon Regional Medical Center. Private practice locations are in San Mateo and San Ramon California.
